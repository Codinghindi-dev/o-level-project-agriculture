ğŸŒ° Types of Seeds

Seeds can be classified based on their source, breeding method, and quality standards:

1. Breeder Seeds

These are genetically pure seeds produced by scientists or breeders. They serve as the base material for producing foundation and certified seeds.

2. Foundation Seeds

Produced from breeder seeds under strict supervision to maintain purity and quality. These are used to produce certified seeds.

3. Certified Seeds

Certified by authorized agencies for purity, germination, and quality. These are distributed to farmers for general cultivation.

4. Hybrid Seeds

Produced by crossing two genetically different plants to combine desirable traits like high yield and pest resistance. Example: Hybrid maize, rice, and vegetables.
