  • 3. Use Organic Manure: Apply compost or cow dung to enrich the soil naturally.
  • 4. Avoid Overuse of Chemicals: Excess fertilizer or pesticide use can damage soil microorganisms.
  • 5. Plant Leguminous Crops: Grow crops like beans or peas to increase nitrogen in the soil.

  • 4. Rainwater Harvesting: Collect and store rainwater for irrigation during dry seasons.
  • 5. Regular Maintenance: Check pipes and sprinklers for leaks or blockages.
